Store your D&D character sheet in the cloud. Draconis Personae is a full-stack Express app, using MongoDB/mLab, Mongoose, and Node.js.

OutYet

Check the most recent episode of your favorite TV show. OutYet uses the TVMaze and TasteDive APIs, and is written using HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript/jQuery.
